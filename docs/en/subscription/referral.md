# Referral program

Tap **Referral System** in the bot's main menu — a screen opens with your personal referral link.

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: referral screen with link]**

## How it works

1. Send your personal link to a friend.
2. Your friend opens the bot through it — this automatically links their account to you as their referrer. The link is only made once, the first time they open the bot through your link, and never changes after that.
3. As soon as your friend subscribes to a paid plan (PRO or ULTIMATE), 5% of that subscription's price is automatically credited to your balance.

## Condition for earning

The bonus is only credited if **you yourself** have paid for a subscription at least once. If you've never bought a subscription yourself, referral earnings from invited friends won't be credited.

## What's next

Earned bonuses land on your balance and can be spent exactly like a regular top-up — see [Subscribing](subscribe.md).
