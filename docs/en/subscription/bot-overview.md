# Getting to know the Telegram bot

Everything related to your subscription — payment, topping up your balance, getting your panel token, the referral program — is handled through a separate Telegram bot, [@divinepanel_bot](https://t.me/divinepanel_bot). The panel itself doesn't connect to this bot directly — they're two different applications: the bot issues a token, and the panel uses that token.

## Opening the bot

Go to [t.me/divinepanel_bot](https://t.me/divinepanel_bot) and tap **Start** (or send the `/start` command).

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: the bot's main screen after /start]**

The main menu opens with the following buttons:

| Button | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Panel Info | Opens the divinepanel.com website |
| Cabinet | Your account — balance, plan, subscription end date |
| Top Up | Ways to top up your balance |
| Guide | Currently still in development — use this guide instead |
| Language | Switches the bot's interface language (Russian/English) |
| Download | Link to download the panel (GitHub) |
| Referral System | Your personal referral link |
| Support | Direct link to contact support |

The **Menu** button at the bottom of almost every screen returns you to this main menu.

## Changing the bot's language

Tap **Language** in the main menu, then choose **RU** or **ENG**. The choice is saved for your account — it won't reset the next time you open the bot.
