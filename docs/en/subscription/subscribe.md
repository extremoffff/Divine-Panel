# Subscribing

You can subscribe or renew either through **Cabinet → Subscribe**, or straight from the **Top Up** flow — after topping up, just head back to Cabinet.

## How to subscribe

1. In Cabinet, tap **Subscribe**.
2. Choose a plan — **PRO** or **ULTIMATE**.
3. Confirm the purchase with **Yes**.
4. If your balance covers it, the subscription activates immediately for 30 days. If it doesn't, the bot shows how much is needed and your current balance — top up (see [Topping up your balance](topup.md)) and try again.

## Renewing and switching plans

- **Renewing the same plan** — just subscribe to it again while your current subscription is still active: 30 days are added to whatever time is left, not replacing it.
- **Switching plans (upgrade or downgrade) before your current subscription ends** — payment is charged right away, but the new plan doesn't switch on instantly: it's queued and activates automatically the moment your current, already-paid period ends. The days you already paid for aren't lost. Cabinet will show a "Next plan: …" line while this is pending.

## Auto-renewal

The plan selection screen has an **Auto-renewal** button.

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: auto-renewal screen]**

If enabled: as soon as your subscription's end date arrives, if your balance covers it, the price of the plan that was active right before that moment is charged automatically and your subscription is extended by another 30 days.

If your balance doesn't cover it, your subscription simply ends as usual — nothing extra is charged — and you get a separate notification explaining that auto-renewal failed due to insufficient balance.

You can turn auto-renewal on or off at any time with the same button.

## Subscription reminders

The bot reminds you when your subscription is about to end — 2 days and 1 day before expiry — and lets you know once it has ended. If you have auto-renewal enabled, instead of the usual "subscription ended" message you'll get either "auto-renewal completed" or "couldn't auto-renew — insufficient balance," depending on whether there was enough money.

## What's next

Once you've subscribed, open [Cabinet and subscription management](cabinet.md) to get your panel token.
