# Sell orders

The **Sell orders** tab shows items the selected account already has listed for sale on the Steam Community Market.

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Sell orders tab with a list of active listings]**

At the top, a summary shows the total value and count of this account's active listings.

## Cancelling a listing

Select one or more listings and click **Cancel selected** — the panel unlists the items from Steam. Once removed, an item returns to your regular inventory (the Inventories tab), from which it can be listed again or sent by trade.

## What's next

To have your listed items automatically follow the market price without manual intervention, set up [Reprice](reprice.md).
