# Inventories

The **Steam Manager** section (in the sidebar) works directly with your Steam inventory and the Steam Community Market, without going through a third-party service. Unlike P2P and DMarket, there's no separate preparation (Prepare) step here — the panel uses the Steam session the account already has. Available on the PRO and ULTIMATE plans.

The page has four tabs: **Inventories**, **Sell orders**, **Buy orders**, **Reprice**.

## The shared toolbar

The Inventories, Sell orders, and Buy orders tabs all work with **one account at a time** — pick an account from the list at the top. That same bar also shows:

- The selected account's Steam wallet balance, including a separately shown "pending" amount (proceeds from recent sales that Steam holds for a few days before it's fully available).
- A game switcher.
- A **↻ Refresh** button — re-fetches data from Steam.
- A **Filters** button — filter by availability, item quality, StatTrak/Souvenir (CS2 only).
- A search field by item name.

## The Inventories tab

Shows the selected account's current Steam inventory for the selected game. You can select one or more items (**Select all** / **Select none**) and run an action:

- **Send via trade offer** — sends the selected items via a trade offer to a specified Steam account.
- **List for sale** — lists the selected items for sale on the Steam Community Market (opens a window to set the price).
