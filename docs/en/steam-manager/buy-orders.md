# Buy orders

The **Buy orders** tab shows the selected account's active buy orders on the Steam Community Market — standing requests to buy an item at a price you set, which fill automatically as soon as someone sells at that price or lower.

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Buy orders tab with a list of active orders]**

## Creating an order

Click **+ New buy order**, specify the item, price, and quantity — the panel creates the order through the Steam Community Market.

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: create buy order window]**

Steam caps the total value of active buy orders relative to the account's Steam wallet balance (roughly a tenfold limit). If you try to create an order beyond that limit, the panel shows a clear error explaining the limit was exceeded.

## Cancelling an order

Each active order's card has a cancel button — it cancels the order on Steam right away, and the funds reserved for it return to the wallet balance.

## Transferring orders to another account

The **⇄ Transfer** button lets you duplicate this account's buy orders onto another one of your accounts — handy when you want the same buy requests spread across several Steam wallets.

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: destination account picker for transferring orders]**

When picking a destination account, the list only shows accounts whose Steam wallet uses **the same currency** as the source account — transferring to an account with a different wallet currency isn't possible, and such accounts simply won't appear in the picker.
