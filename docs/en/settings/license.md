# Token and subscription status

The **Token** settings item is the same window described in [Activating your license](../installation/activate-license.md), reachable any time through the settings icon (the gear) in the panel's sidebar → **Token**.

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: settings icon in the sidebar]**

Worth coming back to when:

- You want to check your current subscription status and plan.
- Your subscription was renewed or your plan changed in the Telegram bot — click **CHECK & SAVE** again so the panel picks up the current status.
- You need to activate the panel on a new computer, with a new token (or the same one, after resetting HWID).

The plan status also syncs in the background on its own, no manual action needed — but a manual check is useful if you want to confirm a change took effect right away.

## When a section isn't available on your current plan

If you try to open a feature that needs a higher plan (for example, P2P on the FREE plan, or Parser on PRO), the panel shows a **Subscription required** window with links to divinepanel.com (to review the plans) and to [@divinepanel_bot](https://t.me/divinepanel_bot) (to subscribe).
