# Introduction

Divine Panel is an application for managing large numbers of Steam accounts at once. The panel works with proxies (each account needs its own) and helps you quickly sell items on marketplaces — including Steam itself — and place buy and sell orders.

Supported games: CS2, Dota 2, Rust, TF2.

Supported marketplaces: market.csgo.com, Waxpeer, DMarket, and direct trading through the Steam Community Market (the Steam Manager section).

## Where to start

Working with the panel follows this order:

1. **Subscribe** through the Telegram bot [@divinepanel_bot](https://t.me/divinepanel_bot) — see [Subscription & Purchase](subscription/bot-overview.md).
2. **Download and install the panel** — see [Installing the Panel](installation/download.md).
3. **Enter your token** in the panel and add your Steam accounts — see [Account Manager](accounts/add-single.md).
4. **Prepare your accounts for the marketplaces you need** and **set price thresholds** — see [P2P Marketplaces](p2p/overview.md), [DMarket](dmarket/overview.md), [Steam Manager](steam-manager/inventories.md).

Every following section of this guide covers one step or one feature of the panel in detail — from the bot's very first screen down to the smallest setting in the interface.

## Plans

The panel has three access tiers:

- **FREE** — no subscription needed. Only the Account Manager is available: adding accounts (one at a time or in bulk), Steam Guard codes and confirmations, auto-accept settings for listings/gifts, and copying trade links. Selling tools (P2P, DMarket, Steam Manager, sales history) are not available.
- **PRO** — unlocks repricing on every marketplace (market.csgo, Waxpeer, DMarket), Steam Manager, sales history, and sale notifications through your personal Telegram bot.
- **ULTIMATE** — everything in PRO, plus Parser (cross-marketplace price comparison) and the profit calculator.

If you try to open a section your current plan doesn't cover, the panel shows a window pointing you to the plans on [divinepanel.com](https://divinepanel.com) and to subscribing through [@divinepanel_bot](https://t.me/divinepanel_bot).
