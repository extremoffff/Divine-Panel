# Balances & transfers

The **Balances**, **Money Transfer**, and **PayPass** buttons live on the toolbar of the **Account Manager** page — they all work across every added account at once.

## Balances

Shows each prepared account's balance on every marketplace (market.csgo, Waxpeer, DMarket), plus its Steam wallet balance, all in one list across every account. Kept fresh by a background poll — you don't need to refresh it manually.

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Balances window]**

## Money Transfer

Lets you collect balances from several accounts into one destination — for example, to consolidate small leftover balances scattered across accounts before withdrawing.

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Money Transfer window]**

The same thing can also be done — but not from the panel, from your personal Telegram notifications bot (not to be confused with the subscription bot, @divinepanel_bot) — by moving an account's entire balance to another, or collecting balances from every account at once, using the `/transfer` command. See [Personal Telegram notifications bot](../settings/notifications-bot.md#transfer-command).

## PayPass

Sets market.csgo's payment password on several selected accounts in one go — no need to open each account's edit form individually.
