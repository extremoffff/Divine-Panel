# Sales history

The **Sales history** section (in the sidebar) is a log of every past sale across all your accounts and marketplaces. Available on the PRO and ULTIMATE plans.

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Sales history page]**

## Filters

Above the table, a filter bar offers:

- **All accounts** — filter by a specific account.
- **All markets** — filter by a specific marketplace.
- **From** / **To** — a date range.
- A search field by item name.

The **↻ Refresh** button updates the list manually.

## Summary and table

Above the list, a short summary shows totals for the currently filtered sales. The table itself has columns: item icon, **Item** (name), **Account**, **Market** (marketplace), **Price**, **Time** (when the sale happened).

The list is paginated — page controls are below the table.
