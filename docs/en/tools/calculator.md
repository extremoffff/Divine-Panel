# Profit calculator

The profit calculator works out how much you'll make (or lose) on a "buy on one marketplace, sell on another" flip, accounting for both marketplaces' fees. Available on the ULTIMATE plan only.

## Opening it

There are two ways to open the calculator:

- The **Calculator** button in the sidebar — opens the floating calculator window.
- The **Profit** button on the Items (P2P), DMarket, and Steam Manager pages — opens the automatic thresholds window instead (see [Buy price and profit calculator](../p2p/buy-price-profit.md)), a different feature with a similarly named button — don't confuse the two.

## A window unlike the others

The calculator isn't a page — it's a **floating window** you can drag around the screen by its title bar. It doesn't close when you switch between sections of the panel and stays on screen until you close it (✕) or minimize it (–) to a small icon.

## Calculation fields

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Platform 1 — deposit commission, % | Platform 1's fee for depositing/entering an item (0 if there isn't one) |
| Platform 2 commission — Sell, % | Platform 2's fee for selling |
| Platform 2 commission — Withdrawal, % | Platform 2's fee for withdrawing funds |
| Price on platform 1 (buy) | The buy price on platform 1 |
| Price on platform 2 (sell) | The sell price on platform 2 |

The result — **Profit / Loss** — updates automatically as you type.

The trash-can button next to the "Prices" label clears only the price fields, leaving the commission percentages you entered untouched — handy for quickly recalculating a new pair of prices with the same fees.
