# Preparing an account for a marketplace

Before an account's items start showing up and getting listed in the Items table on market.csgo or Waxpeer, that account needs to be prepared (Prepare) once for that marketplace.

Preparation happens not in the P2P section but on the account's own card in **Account Manager** — that's also where the marketplace toggles live. Full details are in [Account and marketplace settings](../accounts/account-settings.md#preparing-an-account-prepare).

In short:

1. Open **Account Manager**.
2. On the account's card, find the marketplace block (market.csgo or Waxpeer).
3. Click **Prepare** and wait for it to finish.
4. Turn on the marketplace toggle.

After that, the account's items on that marketplace start appearing in the P2P section's Items table, and the panel begins listing and repricing them automatically within your thresholds.
